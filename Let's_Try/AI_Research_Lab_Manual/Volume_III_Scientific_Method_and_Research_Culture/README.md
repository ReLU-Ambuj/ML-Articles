## Volume Objective

By the end of Volume III, you should be able to:

- Structure your daily and weekly life as a research scientist (not a developer)
- Execute the full research loop: idea → hypothesis → experiment → paper → deployment
- Build a self-sustaining open-source ecosystem around your research

## Prerequisites

- Completed or parallel reading of Volume II (especially Section 7 — Research Areas)
- Ability to read and implement papers independently
- At least one completed replication project

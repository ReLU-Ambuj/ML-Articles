# AI Research Lab Manual
## From Zero to a Globally Respected Research Organization

> **Perspective:** Founder · Research Director · CEO · Economist · Systems Engineer · Technology Historian

---

## About This Manual

This is a graduate-to-PhD level, multi-volume reference manual for building an AI Research Lab from absolute zero into a globally competitive research organization over a 10-year horizon.

It is not startup motivation. It is an execution playbook — covering science, engineering, economics, law, and operations.

---

## Reader Profile

- Starting alone, with strong mathematics background
- Can code; willing to study 10–16 hours/day
- Willing to spend years on original research
- Long-term goal: compete with OpenAI, Anthropic, DeepMind, FAIR, xAI
- Prefers capital-efficient, research-first approach
- Goal: sustainable business that funds fundamental research

## How to Use This Manual

1. **Read sequentially** if you are beginning — Volumes I → II → III
2. **Jump to relevant sections** if you have specific needs (compute, funding, hiring)
3. **Feed one section at a time** to your AI assistant for deep content generation
4. Each section README contains: Objectives · Background · Prompts · Deliverables

---

## Prompt Strategy

Each section README contains a **self-contained, detailed prompt** ready to paste into any AI system (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini) to generate deep, PhD-level content for that specific section. No section prompt is generic — each carries its own mathematical depth, case study requirements, and deliverable specifications.

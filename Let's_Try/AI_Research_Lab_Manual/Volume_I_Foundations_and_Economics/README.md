## Volume Objective

By the end of Volume I, you should be able to:

- Precisely define what an AI Research Lab is and which archetype best fits your goals
- Understand the full historical context: why labs succeeded, why they failed, and what shifted economically
- Know exactly where money comes from and how frontier labs stay solvent
- Have a concrete capital plan for ₹50K, ₹5L, and ₹50L starting budgets
- Know who to hire first and when — and why that order matters

## Prerequisites

- Basic familiarity with ML terminology (neural networks, training, inference)
- Awareness of at least 3–4 frontier AI organizations
- Willingness to engage with economic and organizational theory alongside technical content

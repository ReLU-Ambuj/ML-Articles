## Volume Objective

By the end of Volume IV, you should be able to:

- Know precisely when to raise money and when raising money is a mistake
- Understand every legal structure, license type, and compliance obligation relevant to an AI lab
- Choose a business model that funds research sustainably without compromising scientific integrity

## Prerequisites

- Basic understanding of corporate structures (LLC, C-Corp, etc.)
- Awareness of open-source licensing (MIT, Apache, GPL)
- Familiarity with the venture capital funding pipeline

## Volume Objective

By the end of Volume V, you should be able to:

- Understand the technical and philosophical landscape of AI alignment and safety
- Implement practical safety measures in your own models (red teaming, eval frameworks)
- Position your lab credibly in the safety space without sacrificing research velocity

## Prerequisites

- Strong understanding of LLM training (Volumes I–II)
- Familiarity with RLHF and constitutional AI concepts
- Awareness of key safety organizations: ARC, Redwood Research, Apollo Research

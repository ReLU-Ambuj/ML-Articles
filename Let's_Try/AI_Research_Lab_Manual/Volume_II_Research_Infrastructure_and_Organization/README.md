## Volume Objective

By the end of Volume II, you should be able to:

- Design a functional organizational chart that scales from 1 to 100+ people
- Identify which research areas are frontier, which are saturated, and which are open
- Spec out an end-to-end ML infrastructure stack from scratch
- Make informed compute purchase vs. cloud decisions with cost models
- Build legally compliant, high-quality datasets
- Navigate the publication pipeline from idea to accepted paper at a top venue

## Prerequisites

- Completed Volume I (or equivalent understanding of lab archetypes and economics)
- Familiarity with Python, PyTorch, and basic distributed systems concepts
- Basic understanding of academic publishing norms
